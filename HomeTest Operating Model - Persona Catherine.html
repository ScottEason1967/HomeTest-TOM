Portrait commissioning
Why Catherine is here Sarah was originally framed as a single universal commissioner across HIV (LA), PSA-AS (Acute Trust) and HbA1c (ICB / GP). That collapses three different institutions doing three different jobs into one person, and it doesn't hold. Catherine is the Acute Trust commissioner persona separated out from Sarah. She is anchored on the PSA Active Surveillance established radiotherapy-follow-up cohort, which is Mark's cohort. Sarah remains as the LA sexual health commissioner for HIV. Ruth stays as the PSA-AS monitoring-gap commissioner (TBC). Anita is the ICB / GP commissioner for HomeTest HbA1c.

What Catherine needs from the framework

Three things. First, a tariff envelope she can plan against: the PSA-AS Monitoring Episode tariff under design has to land with a glide-path her Trust's finance team can take to board. Second, a contract route that survives audit: acute trust commissioners commission through the Standard Contract architecture, and the call-off mechanism has to slot inside it. Third, an end-to-end clinical pathway her consultants will actually adopt — that's where Adam sits.

5Served

The output side. Who HomeTest sells to and serves

The customers and end users of the operating model. Three audiences with different relationships to the platform: the commissioners who buy it, the patients who use it, and the clinicians (GPs, consultants and prospectively pharmacists) who initiate testing as the upstream of the patient pathway.

Commissioners
LAs, ICBs, Acute Trusts, NHSE programmes. Buy via the framework, run live configurations locally. Represented by Sarah in the cast. Direction in flight: the platform brokers reach and targeting commissioners cannot replicate themselves (UKHSA data, NHSE social, Nextdoor, LSOA-level segmentation), moving the offer beyond passive plumbing.
Patients
Abdul, Mark, Layla and the archetypes that follow. The end users. The reason the model exists.
Initiating clinicians In flight
GPs, consultants and (prospectively) community pharmacists who initiate testing. GP-init and clinician-init are live patterns; pharmacy-init is a fourth initiation pattern currently subject to an open SLT scope decision. Not directly contracted by HomeTest in any case; they are the upstream of the patient pathway. Status: GP-init and clinician-init are confirmed initiation patterns. Pharmacy-init is subject to an open SLT scope decision.

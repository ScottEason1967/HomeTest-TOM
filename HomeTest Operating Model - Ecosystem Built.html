Ecosystem · Category 1

1Built and run by HomeTest

What HomeTest builds, runs and staffs directly

Eleven elements make up the core that HomeTest builds, runs or stewards. Some sit with the Product Team at delivery level; some sit with the Sponsor as holder of the blueprint; some stay with the HomeTest team as the Pathway 2 hypothesis owner. Everything else in the ecosystem sits outside the team.

Integration spine In flight
The orchestration layer that routes ordering, dispatch, results and status across the ecosystem. Status: live for HIV; scaling for PSA and iron deficiency anaemia configurations as they enter scope. Owner: Head of Technology & Product.
Framework instrument In flight
Commercial vehicle and call-off architecture. Admits, contracts and exits suppliers. Status: two-track recommendation in the Recommendation paper: Plan A PA23 Open Framework is the lead position, confirmed at the 22 June 2026 SLT playback (the 18 June external procurement legal counsel workshop fed the decision). Six-month NHS SBS Sexual Health Framework (SBS10255) as interim for HIV. Confirmed at the 22 June 2026 SLT Leeds playback. Owner: Commercial Operations with NHSE Commercial and NHS Legal.
Pattern catalogue In flight
Library of reusable overlay options across initiation, commissioning, supply chain and the rest. Status: live for HIV patterns; PSA and iron deficiency anaemia patterns being added; pharmacy-init pattern subject to an open SLT scope decision. Owner: Product Manager.
Test catalog In flight
The assurance and discoverability layer: which tests, which providers, which pathways, with the SNOMED codes that make them addressable in the wider NHS data estate. The catalog is what lets commissioners and clinicians find a test and trust it has been admitted. It is not a marketplace. There is no purchase or transact function inside it; buying happens through the framework. Status: principles to be ratified at programme level. Gap analysis against the FDP genomics catalog outstanding. Owner: Head of Technology & Product with Clinical Safety Lead.
Configurations In flight
Live deployments of specific pattern combinations: HIV via LA, PSA via acute trust, iron deficiency anaemia via GP. Status: HIV configuration live in private beta; PSA configuration in design; iron deficiency anaemia configuration in discovery. Owner: Product Manager with Commercial Operations and NHSE Commercial.
IG framework structure In flight
Federated multi-controller model and the artefact stack (DPIA, DSA, DPA, IAR) every configuration inherits. Status: federated multi-controller model agreed in principle. Lawful basis position open at SLT level. IAO appointed (permanent NHSE employee, per PTT requirement; confirmed at the June 2026 Delivery Board), the earlier critical-blocker status is closed. Owner: IG & Assurance SME.
Clinical safety case structure
Hazard log structure, SLSP template, configuration sign-off route. Owner: Clinical Safety Lead.
Hazard log structure
The structure into which configuration-specific hazards are logged and reviewed. Owner: Clinical Safety Lead.
Onboarding gateway In flight
The seven-stage supplier journey from EOI to admission. Status: in operation for Alpha suppliers; Beta evolution adds the public market engagement and full seven-stage flow. Owner: Commercial Operations with NHSE Commercial, Clinical Safety Lead and IG & Assurance SME.
MI and data flow integrity In flight
Platform-level reporting, anomaly detection, access controls, audit logs. Status: live for HIV; KPI framework not yet designed; outcome attribution methodology undefined. Owner: Product Manager with IG & Assurance SME.
Future State Blueprint In flight
Holds the Sponsor's vision, the current thinking, and the open decisions to land it. HomeTest team stewards the Pathway 2 hypothesis; commercial workstream sits with the Product Team.Owner: Sponsor.

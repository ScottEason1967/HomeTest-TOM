Commercial sourcing strategy

Milestone Plan.

In development
The commercial sourcing strategy milestone plan is being developed for the upcoming workshop. It is not yet ready for distribution.

What this page will hold. The end-to-end commercial milestone plan sequenced through the five procurement stages: planning, pre-procurement governance, procurement process, evaluation and award, and post-procurement governance.

The plan will set out the two routes running in parallel. Plan A is the long-term NHSE-administered Open Framework under PA23. Plan C is the commissioner-led interim that protects the April 2027 HIV Beta commitment. The dovetail with the operating model stand-up sits underneath both, per Section 14 of the recommendation paper.

A draft version will be circulated ahead of the workshop sessions for comment.

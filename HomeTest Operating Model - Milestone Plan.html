Commercial sourcing strategy

Milestone Plan.

The commercial sourcing strategy milestone plan is in development. The working view is being prepared for the upcoming workshop and is not yet ready for general distribution.
What this page will hold. The end-to-end commercial milestone plan sequenced through the five procurement stages: planning, pre-procurement governance, procurement process, evaluation and award, and post-procurement governance. The plan threads together the procurement spine, the decisions sitting with SLT, the inbound dependencies that gate commercial work, and the critical path the timetable hangs from.

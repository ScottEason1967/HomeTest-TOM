Anita.

ICB / GP commissioner · HomeTest iron deficiency anaemia
“Show me where the money sits, who carries the clinical pathway and how it ties to the QOF cycle. Until you do, my ICB can't commit.”
Anita is the ICB / GP commissioner who holds the budget for HomeTest iron deficiency anaemia — the GP-initiated primary-care anaemia monitoring configuration. Layla sits inside her cohort. Her institution is either an ICB-level commissioner with primary-care reach or a GP-practice commissioner inside the ICB; the exact split is design-phase. Her route depends on the QOF / outpatient tariff position and the primary-care contractual mechanism, neither of which is available within the 2027/28 tariff window.
Why Anita is here Sarah was originally framed as a single universal commissioner across HIV (LA), PSA-AS (Acute Trust) and iron deficiency anaemia (ICB / GP). Three institutions, three jobs — not one person. Anita is the ICB / GP commissioner persona separated out from Sarah. She is anchored on the HomeTest iron deficiency anaemia configuration, which is Layla's pathway. Sarah remains as the LA sexual health commissioner for HIV. Catherine is the Acute Trust commissioner for the PSA-AS established cohort. Ruth is the PSA-AS monitoring-gap commissioner (TBC).

What Anita needs from the framework

Three things. First, a viable funding route at pathway level: QOF, outpatient tariff or new primary-care contractual mechanism — the primary-care contractual mechanism is not available within the 2027/28 window, so the route has to land before iron deficiency anaemia can scale. Second, IG that fits the GP-record integration shape (FHIR write-back, DSA between supplier and GP practice). Third, a buy mechanism that fits ICB-level commissioning and doesn't require every GP practice to sign separately.

Where Anita appears in the model

  • Operational swimlane Lane 1 (Commissioner buying), as one of four commissioner personas in the cluster
  • Around Layla's persona page as the commissioner for her pathway
The role threads across; the person does not. Anita is one of several commissioner personas across the portfolio. Each configuration has its own commissioner institution. The framework has to work for all four (Sarah, Catherine, Ruth, Anita); the commissioner role is the same role, the institutions are different.

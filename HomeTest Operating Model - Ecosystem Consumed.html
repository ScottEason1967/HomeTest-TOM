Ecosystem · Category 2

2Consumed from NHS infrastructure

Shared NHS services HomeTest plugs into

Configured and paid for per use, not built or run. HomeTest interfaces with these services; the services are owned and operated by their NHS counterparts to their own SLAs. Six services make up this layer today. Notify scope is currently under live review: whether it extends into physical fulfilment, kit dispatch and sample handling is an open question with a mid-June target for a response.

NHS Login
Patient identity and authentication. HomeTest interfaces; the service is owned and run by NHS Login. Owner: Product Manager.
NHS Notify In flight
Patient communications today. HomeTest configures templates and channels; Notify operates the comms infrastructure. Status: scope under active review. Open question on whether Notify also covers physical fulfilment, kit dispatch, return logistics and sample handling. Treat as comms only until the position lands; do not assume fulfilment in scope. Owner: Product Manager with Commercial Operations and NHSE Commercial.
NHS App In flight
The patient digital surface. HomeTest presents through it; the App team owns the surface. Status: working partnership with the NHS App test-results team on native rendering of HomeTest results. Interim is a web hand-off into a HomeTest-hosted results UI. Longer-term target is the Personal Data Model (PDM) as the canonical record of test results, with the NHS App as the native render against it. Replaces the IM1 aggregator pattern over time. Owner: Product Manager.
FHIR routing
Clinical data exchange across NHS systems. HomeTest interfaces; routing is owned centrally. Owner: Product Manager.
Federated Data Platform In flight
Analytics backbone where in scope. HomeTest aligns to FDP governance; FDP is run separately. Status: alignment in flight. FDP governance and lawful basis are being worked through where HomeTest configurations need analytics integration. Position depends on configuration scope. Owner: Product Manager with IG & Assurance SME.
Single Patient Record In flight
Canonical result destination where configuration requires. HomeTest writes; SPR owns the record. Status: alignment in flight per configuration. SPR governance defines what result destinations and what controller designation applies; HomeTest aligns rather than creates parallel governance. Owner: Product Manager with IG & Assurance SME.

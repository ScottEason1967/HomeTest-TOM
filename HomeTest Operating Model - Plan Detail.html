Milestone Plan.

Commercial milestone plan for the HomeTest framework, a PA23 single-stage Open Procedure. The Plan A spine runs to Go Live on 1 August 2027. The Plan C interim route runs in parallel to protect the April 2027 launch, and is now live through the NHS Online IDA commission. The milestone plan, with Plan C now live

This is the current Sourcing Strategy Milestone Plan for the HomeTest framework, a PA23 single-stage Open Procedure, updated 20 July 2026. The roadmap and matrix that follow carry these dates. Plan C has moved from a design idea to a live commission: on 3 July Martin committed the programme to support NHS Online's procurement of self-sample iron deficiency anaemia (IDA) testing, the first real commissioner demand against the Plan A plus Plan C architecture agreed on 22 June.

Milestone dates · Plan A spine
  • Requirements gathering complete, 15 Oct 26
  • PIN, Planned Procurement Notice, 1 Sep 26
  • Sourcing strategy finalised, 30 Sep 26
  • Tender notice and ITT out, 5 Jan 27, tender window to 19 Feb 27
  • Evaluation and moderation, to 31 Mar 27
  • Award, 30 Jun 27
  • Contract signature, by 30 Jul 27
  • Go Live, 1 Aug 27
Four swimlanes run in parallel from July: Preparation (1 Jul to 15 Oct 26), ITT development (3 Aug 26 to 31 Mar 27), Approvals OBC (13 Jul to 1 Dec 26) and Contract FBC (1 Apr to 30 Jul 27). Every activity carries a Commercial or Programme lead with the other in support. Governance gates: GAS/GAtS Oct 26, CEG 10 Nov 26 and DHSC CAB 1 Dec 26 on the OBC side; NHSE Atamis, CEG 11 May 27 and DHSC CAB 1 Jun 27 on the FBC side.
Plan C live · NHS Online IDA commission

Two work packages off Martin's 3 July commitment. WP1 is the service design and UX response (service and product teams, first cut close of play 9 July). WP2 is the tactical procurement, owned by Commercial, docked to the Interim Route to Market Approach line, 13 to 31 Jul 26. NHS Online is the contracting authority. Launch is planned for April 2027, with January treated only as an accelerated scenario.

WP2 deliverables
  • Route options note, existing framework call-off versus open tender under PA23, with a recommendation. Due 10 Jul, worked through at the 8 July Leeds workshops.
  • Draft procurement process, end to end on the confirmed route, back-calculated from launch. Due 31 Jul.
  • Tender specification requirements, the information set the spec must contain, built on the 9 July service design baseline. Due 31 Jul.
  • Venepuncture watch brief, a contact into NHS Online's venepuncture procurement and its NDS interface. Standing.
  • Strategic alignment, Plan C interim and Plan A framework told as one story, checkpointed at Programme Board on 20 Jul.

Two caveats carried on the plan: the RAG is a green baseline only, no RAG data is held yet; and the roles on the final two FBC gates are inferred, not settled.

Costs and benefits of home testing sit in different parts of the local budget. Pathology networks don't run phlebotomy: that sits under clinical services or the ICB.

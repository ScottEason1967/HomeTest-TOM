WHAT

What it is composed of, and the world it sits in.

Two lenses on the model. Pick one.

Most operating models try to describe what they are by listing their internal parts. That is true but partial. The model is also defined by what surrounds it: the services it consumes, the bodies that regulate it, the suppliers it contracts, the audiences it serves. Anatomy tells you the inside. Ecosystem tells you the outside. Both matter.

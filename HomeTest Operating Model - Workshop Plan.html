HOMETEST OPERATING MODEL

Workshop programme

Structured working sessions distributed across the five procurement stages, each anchored to a specific decision or output that needs to land in that stage. Ownership of each session and its outputs sits on the programme side; the commercial team attends and consumes the outputs into the sourcing strategy. Every workshop is a click-through with a full agenda: attendees, prep required, objectives, decisions to land and outputs.

Core group · attends every workshop

Each workshop’s subject matter and outputs are owned on the programme side; owners are being confirmed ahead of the 13 August foundation workshop and every session below is flagged in red until its owner is named. The anchor group attends every session for continuity, because the sourcing strategy consumes the outputs to fixed dates. Additional specialists are pulled in only where the session content calls for it. Whether the full anchor group attends every 2-hour session is for the SLT to confirm.

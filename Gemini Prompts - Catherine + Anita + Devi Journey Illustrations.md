# Gemini image prompts — Catherine, Anita, Devi Journeys

All images to match the existing Sarah/Adam/Layla journey illustration style: soft editorial illustration, muted mid-tones, warm skin light, NHS blue and gold accents (deep navy #003087, warm amber/gold, soft off-white paper backgrounds), semi-flat with light shadowing, no photographic realism, no logos, no on-image text, no watermarks. All figures rendered at approachable working-professional register — not glamour, not corporate stock. Faces readable but not identifying.

Aspect ratio for stage illustrations: 16:9 landscape, 1600 × 900 minimum.
Aspect ratio for portraits: 1:1 square, 1024 × 1024, centred bust with soft background.
Aspect ratio for interlude: 16:9 landscape, atmospheric spacer image.

Save each file to the persona's Story folder using the naming convention shown.

## Style notes across all four sets

- **Diversity respected without tokenism.** Faces and body language grounded in real working-adult NHS demographics.
- **No NHS logos, no supplier logos, no branded interface chrome.** Interfaces are suggestive, not literal.
- **No on-image text of any kind.** All narrative sits in the HTML copy.
- **Palette continuity with existing images:** deep NHS navy #003087 for institutional register, warm amber/gold for accent, off-white paper backgrounds, warm skin lighting, muted teal and terracotta as secondary tones. Avoid pure black and pure white.
- **Composition register:** editorial illustration, semi-flat with light shading; not photo-real; not corporate flat-vector.
- **Emotional register:** competent, calm, quietly serious. No triumphalism. No stress. This is the operating model working.
- **Delivery:** PNG with transparent background NOT required — solid, semi-blended backgrounds are the correct style.

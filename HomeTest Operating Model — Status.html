Pharmacy-led prescribing
Whether pharmacy-initiated testing belongs in scope as a fourth initiation pattern. Affects supply chain, accreditation, sign-off and result handling design.
Decision: SLT.
Open on CONTRACT →
Cost-benefit dislocation at local system level
Costs and benefits of home testing sit in different parts of the local budget. Pathology networks don't run phlebotomy: that sits under clinical services or the ICB. When home testing replaces venepuncture, the saving lands in one budget line and the new spend lands in another. Without best-practice tariffs at pathway level, ICBs see home testing as a cost pressure rather than a value case. The economic case is the unlock for adoption.
Decision: SLT with NHSE strategic finance. Health Economist input required.
